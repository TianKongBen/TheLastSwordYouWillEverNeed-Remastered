## Legendary Return
The mythic blade makes its triumphant comeback! "The Last Sword You Will Ever Need: Remastered" faithfully recreates the classic 1.7.10 experience and brings it to version 1.20.1!

**Original Author:** [QueenofSquiggles](https://www.curseforge.com/members/queenofsquiggles)

## Original Mod Content

### The Last Sword You Will Ever Need
- **Crafting Recipe:** Dragon Egg + Diamond Blocks


![ ](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/5a481049ea7866cce56bef480422bd7fbf839d35.png)
- **11 Upgrade Tiers** (Max Damage: 7,999,999,874,453,995,500)

![ ](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/8e06aacddcd565765993be02b37a27e3fe093d86.png)
- **Features:**
    - Instant Mining (breaks most blocks nearly instantly)
    - Right-click to summon diamonds (triggers lightning strikes when touching entities/blocks)
      *Note: Due to game mechanics, rapid lightning strikes may destroy nearby drops*

### Dragon Crystal
- 1 Dragon Egg → 9 Dragon Crystals

![ ](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/4674803c46c7e558d9e100bdc98bf2a3c691196e.png)
- Core crafting material for all mod items

### Dragon Crystal Armor
- Average stats when worn individually
- **Full set bonus grants:**
    - Night Vision
    - Haste
    - Strength
    - Resistance
    - Flight ability

### Blocks
1. **Compressed Star:**
    - Crafted from 4 Nether Stars

   ![ ](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/2359faa9e2609a5cb2a661fcebd78ca18a24dbb5.png)
    - Features:
        - Light Level 14
        - Instantly mineable by hand
        - Used to craft Dragon Eggs (with Diamond Blocks and Obsidian)
          ![ ](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/0f370ce15c26730cc51cb77e55a0bc31ba88adbd.png)

2. **Dragon Crystal Blocks:**
    - Includes: Dragon Crystal Block, Dragon Crystal Ore
    - Features:
        - Emits dragon roars when interacted with/stepped on
        - Dragon Crystal Ore uses the same texture as Stone
        - Ore drops 3-5 Dragon Crystals (must be placed manually)

*All mod items are added to vanilla creative tabs:*

## 原模组内容

### 最终之剑
- **合成配方：** 龙蛋 + 钻石块

![ ](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/f212b4c86f7565b359e8fc6864d4d2464c3a7197.png)
- **特性：**
- 极速挖掘（几乎瞬间破坏大多数方块）
- 右键召唤钻石（触碰实体/方块时引发闪电攻击）

- 1个龙蛋 → 9个龙晶

- 单件属性普通
- **全套穿戴后获得：**
- 夜视
- 急迫
- 力量
- 抗性提升
- 飞行能力

    - 合成：4个下界之星
